I have decided to keep things light as possible so that I can transport them everywhere. After a nice cozy cup of coffee and some honey toast, I think I'm good to go.
  • Its 9:30 am, and now I am officially boarding the flight bound to Tokyo. What a wonderful sight it is out the window, the morning sunshine brightly illuminating the interior, instantly brightening my mood
  • Its 9:50am.The All Nippon airways plane takes off into the air. What a magnificent view it is out the window, although the lighting may be a little dark, but I think its clear enough. The clouds look like an inviting cotton pillow that you can rest your head on
  • 10:30am.I'm loving the in-flight entertainment here. Its just so full of wonderful movies like Diary of a Whimpy Kid, It, Pet Semetary, or TV shows like Westworld! The Attendant is serving delightful portions of lunch which looks mouthwatering and appealing. Well, time to dig in, I guess!
  • Its 10:00 PM as the plane had finally landed on Narita Airport Tokyo. Boy, am I exhausted but the entertainment made it so enjoyable
  • 3PM:After a nice 12 hour break of me being knocked out, I made a 4 hour journey in the morning to look at one of the most appealing sights in Japan- Mount Fuji. Its not only a stupendous sight but the Japanese also consider it a sacred place to pay respects
  • After that stunning sight and a nice meal in traditional Japanese style, I made sure to journey back to Tokyo and visit its metropolitean areas to shop for some souvenirs. I cannot believe they have such snacks like a chocolate covered banana!

My encounters with a stray kitty!

6/20/2021

Today was a normal day. But as they said, boring is good. I had however, met a stray kitty outside my front door and laying on my doorstep at the early hours of the morning.

Naturally, my instinct would be to pet and feed it, but I was anxious to return the poor creature back to its owner, and I thus brought it to the animal shelter.

The look on the owner's face was priceless as he hugged the kitty with tears in his eyes. Ah, my heart was so filled with happiness after seeing the two of them together, although I did want to get a pet myself.
